## Problem Statement

- Multiple legal entities (Company Codes)
- Thousands of invoices per month
- Mix of PO-based and non-PO invoices
- Complex approval rules depending on amount, organisation and country

The AP team needs a standardised, auditable and measurable process.

## Goals

- Reduce average processing time from invoice receipt to posting
- Increase share of invoices processed before Due Date
- Reduce manual work and number of exceptions
- Provide management with clear KPIs and dashboards

## Scope of This Project

In scope:

- Process design (BPMN) for AP workflow
- Data model (SQL Server) for invoices and approvals
- Reporting layer (Power BI) for AP KPIs

Out of scope:

- Full ERP integration
- Real OCR and email ingestion
- User interface for end users
